About
Obinna

Prince Obinna Lawrence Nwosu was born on the 13th December 1990 to the family of His Royal Majesty Eze Sir Uzo Nwosu JP and Ugoeze Professor Ngozi Nwosu JP (Umukabia Autonomous community, Abia state). He was born in Sokoto state, where he had most of his primary and secondary education. He has a BSc Mathematics degree from Nasarawa State University, and served in Edo state. He proceeded to England for his MSc in Investment and Finance. On returning to Nigeria 2015, he had a stint in the private sector before deciding that his future was in public service.
